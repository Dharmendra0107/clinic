Our Departments

Cardiology

Providing comprehensive cardiac care, from prevention to complex surgeries. Our team of experts ensures top-quality treatment for all heart conditions.

Orthopedics

Advanced treatment options for bone, joint, and muscle conditions. We specialize in both surgical and non-surgical methods to restore mobility and relieve pain.

Pediatrics

Compassionate care for children, from infancy through adolescence. Our pediatricians are dedicated to the well-being and development of young patients.

Special Programs

Heart Health Program

Comprehensive packages for cardiac health, including screenings, lifestyle coaching, and personalized treatment plans designed to improve heart health.

Mother & Child Care

Specialized care packages for mothers and their newborns, including prenatal and postnatal care, ensuring a healthy start for both mother and baby.

MRI & CT Imaging

Advanced imaging technologies providing accurate diagnostics with high-definition clarity, allowing for precise and effective treatment planning.
